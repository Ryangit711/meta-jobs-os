## What Consulting Firms Actually Screen For

### The 5 Gates (Every Big4/MBB/Boutique Uses These)

| Gate | What They Test | What They DON'T Test |
|------|---------------|---------------------|
| **1. Resume Screen** | Impact stories, metrics, progression arc | GPA, university name, class rank |
| **2. Case Interview** | Structured thinking, hypothesis generation, communication | Math speed, accounting knowledge, specific formulas |
| **3. Fit Interview** | Self-awareness, leadership narrative, EQ | Whether you went to Ivey/Queens |
| **4. Written Case** | Synthesis, hypothesis-driven recommendations | Length, formatting polish |
| **5. Reference Check** | Do people you worked with say you're excellent? | Credentials, pedigree |

### What They Claim vs. What They Mean

| Claim | Translation |
|-------|-------------|
| "We hire from top schools" | Our campus recruiting pipeline runs through top schools. Experienced hires come from ANYWHERE. |
| "Strong analytical skills required" | Can you take a messy problem, structure it, and communicate a recommendation? Not can you do calculus. |
| "Previous consulting experience preferred" | We'd prefer someone who already knows the format. But if you can case well and have real operational experience, you're MORE valuable than career consultants. |
| "MBA required" | Soft requirement for senior roles. Your de facto COO experience + distance MBA + post-bacc = clears this gate. |
