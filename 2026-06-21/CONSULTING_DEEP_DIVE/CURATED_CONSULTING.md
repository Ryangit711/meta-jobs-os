## Consulting Interview Process (What to Expect)

| Stage | What Happens | Duration | Prep Needed |
|-------|-------------|----------|-------------|
| 1. Resume Screen | ATS + recruiter reviews. Keywords: M&A, strategy, operations, transformation, healthcare | 3-7 days | Resume already optimized for consulting vocab |
| 2. Recruiter Phone Screen | 30 min. "Why consulting?", "Why Deloitte?", "Walk me through your resume" | 30 min | Practice "why consulting" story |
| 3. Case Interview (1st) | 45 min business case. Typically: profitability, market entry, growth strategy | 45 min | 10 practice cases minimum |
| 4. Case Interview (2nd) | More complex case OR same format with partner | 45 min | Same prep |
| 5. Partner Interview | Fit + behavioral. "Tell me about a time..." | 45 min | STAR framework |
| 6. Offer/Reject | Decision within 1 week of final round | — | — |

**Total timeline:** 3-6 weeks from application to offer.

**Big4 vs MBB difference:** Big4 (Deloitte, EY, KPMG, PwC) has less intense case interviews than MBB (McKinsey, BCG, Bain). You're targeting Big4 first — this is the easier entry point.
