# Consulting — Is It the Fastest/Easiest Way In?

## The Question
*"Emphasize consulting. Check if it's the fastest, easiest way in."*

Answer: **No, but it's the highest-leverage way in.** Here's what that means.

---

## What "Fastest" Actually Means

**Fastest overall:** T Pipe (tech) — Indeed Sr. Manager is already SHOT. Submit today, callback in days.

**Fastest consulting:** Big4 experienced hire (Deloitte, EY, KPMG) — year-round recruiting, no campus cycle wait. But still 3-5 interview rounds over 3-6 weeks.

**Fastest money:** Methanex Director Strategy ($195K-$252K) — one direct application, corporate interview process, higher salary than any consulting Sr. Manager role in Canada.

## What "Easiest" Actually Means

**Easiest entry:** Indeed — already built the package, just submit from phone. 10 minutes.

**Easiest consulting entry:** Deloitte (largest, most structured experienced hire program) or KPMG (smallest Vancouver office, less competition).

**Easiest overall:** Hiive Chief of Staff — startup founder chat, no case interview, no multi-round gauntlet.

**Do consulting, but don't do consulting only.**

Apply to Indeed TODAY (10 minutes). Then SHOOT Methanex Director Strategy (highest $, simplest process). Then prepare 10 consulting cases while waiting for callbacks.
