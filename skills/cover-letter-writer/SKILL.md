# COVER LETTER WRITER — DNA-Alchemized Narrative Engine

## Source Truth Anchors
- Infiltration layer: `37_INFILTRATION_LAYER.md` (JOBS-OS)
- Cover letter templates: `12_COVER_LETTERS.md` (JOBS-OS)
- Agnostic framing: `03_AGNOSTIC_FRAMING.md` (JOBS-OS)

## Format (Mandatory)

### Header Block
```
[NAME]
Date: DD/MM/YYYY
Vancouver, BC, Canada
```

### Body — 6-Paragraph Narrative Arc
1. **Hook** — Specific reason you're writing. Reference company by name. Show you know what they do.
2. **Why them** — What you admire about their specific approach/mission/product. Use THEIR language.
3. **Your parallel** — The real experience that maps to their needs. Not "I did X." "I solved the exact problem you're describing."
4. **Proof** — Specific metric-driven bullet (woven into prose, not a list). Use their value system's framing.
5. **Fit** — Why you're the right person for THIS team at THIS stage. Reference their culture.
6. **Close** — Call to action. Confident, not desperate. "I'd welcome the chance to show [HM name] how I'd approach [their specific challenge]."

### Closing
```
Best regards,
[NAME]
Phone: [PHONE]
Email: [EMAIL] (clickable mailto:)
LinkedIn: [LINKEDIN] (clickable https://)
```

## Rules
- Every letter is UNIQUE to that company. No recycled phrases. No fill-in-the-blank.
- Company DNA language woven throughout — their verbs, their nouns, their framing
- No immigration/PR/work permit language
- No generic "I am writing to apply for..."
- Email and LinkedIn must be clickable hyperlinks in DOCX
- Max 1.5 pages
- Passes the HUMAN test — read aloud, sounds like a person wrote it

## Output
1. Plain text (code fence) for proofreading
2. LaTeX source (code fence) with hyperlinks
