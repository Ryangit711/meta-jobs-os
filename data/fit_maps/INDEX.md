# FIT MAP — Company Ontology System

## What This Is

An abstract ranking system mapping **Aman vs. Company** (how well his profile matches their DNA) AND **Company vs. Aman** (what the company offers him). This is a permanent system component — not tied to any date or wave.

## The Two Scores

| Score | What It Measures | Range |
|-------|-----------------|-------|
| **A→C** | Aman → Company. How well his Director-level operations + M&A + systems-building background aligns with what THIS company needs from THIS role. | 1.0–10.0 |
| **C→A** | Company → Aman. How well this company meets Aman's needs: salary, growth trajectory, brand value, culture fit, location, work-life balance, exit optionality. | 1.0–10.0 |

## Combined Score (Priority Index)

```
Priority = (A→C × 0.6) + (C→A × 0.4)
```

Weighted 60% toward fit (you must be what they need) and 40% toward what they offer you (it must be worth your time).
