## Caption
I got a corporate job in Canada in 1 month when I stopped applying randomly… and started applying strategically.👩🏻‍💻🇨🇦

Here’s what I changed:

• Instead of just clicking “Easy Apply” on LinkedIn, I started reaching out to hiring managers and people already working in the domain I wanted.

• I tracked every single job I applied to.
3 categories: In Process | Rejected | Interview
It gave me clarity and control instead of anxiety.

• I gave myself a deadline — I had to get a job before graduation.
I applied to 8–9 jobs every day, but focused on quality > quantity.

• I used AI tools (like Overleaf-style resume tools) to tailor my resume every single time according to the job description.

• I spoke to people on LinkedIn and even Instagram to understand their experience and get real tips.

• Before every interview, I did proper research — the company’s history, goals, values, recent achievements.
HRs LOVE when you actually know who they are.

If you’re job hunting in Canada right now, stop mass applying and start applying smart.
