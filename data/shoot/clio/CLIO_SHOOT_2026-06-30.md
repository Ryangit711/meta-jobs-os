**Q1: Walk me through how you'd approach a new market opportunity for Clio.**
*Aman's answer:* "I'd start with the same framework I used when evaluating expansion into new US states: size the market first (TAM, SAM, SOM), assess the competitive landscape (who's winning and why), understand the customer segment (law firm size, practice area, geography), and model the unit economics (CAC, LTV, payback period). **Q2: How do you measure the success of a strategy & operations function?**
*Aman's answer:* "Three metrics: (1) Decision velocity — how quickly can leadership make informed decisions? I measure this by the time from question to board-ready analysis. Throughout the year, I'd build a business review cadence that tracks progress against those OKRs and provides an early warning system when we're off track. **Q5: Why Clio? Why this role?**
*Aman's answer:* "Three reasons. First, Clio is at an inflection point — legal AI is transforming how law firms operate, and Clio is leading that transformation.
