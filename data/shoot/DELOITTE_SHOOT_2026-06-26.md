**Title:** From Operator to Advisor: What Building a Business Teaches About Transforming One

For nine years, I built something from nothing. A healthcare business that started with a single location and three people grew into a multi-state operation of 120 employees across five sites, delivering $4M in annual revenue. Along the way, I learned something that no case study could teach: organizational transformation is not about slides and frameworks. When I read Deloitte's description of the Strategic & Technology Change practice — helping leaders navigate complex transformations and implement change management programs to achieve sustainable business and human outcomes — I recognized my own story. I have been the leader on the other side of that transformation. Now I want to be the advisor who helps others navigate it.
